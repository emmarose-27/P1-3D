Global Historical Earthquake Archive (since 1900)

The US Geological Survey maintains official records of earthquake activity from around the globe. This application displays all earthquakes since 1900 with a magnitude 4.0 or greater, and updates once a day. In this visualization, small yellow spheres (
) are earthquakes with magnitudes between 4 and 6. Large red spheres (
) are earthquakes with a magnitude higher than 7. In between, the color and size of spheres are interpolated. Earthquake depth is exaggerated by a factor of 6. The pink lines (
) represent the boundaries of the tectonic plates. Earthquake data from USGS. Country borders feature service is made available by the Living Atlas. Visualization created with Esri's ArcGIS API for JavaScript.
